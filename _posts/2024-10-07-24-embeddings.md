The Newtonian gravitational potential is defined as

\begin{equation}
\phi = - \frac{G M}{r} .
\end{equation}

\begin{equation} \label{test}
\phi = - \frac{G M}{r} .

Refering to equation \ref{test}

We can find the gravitational field by taking the gradient of the potential

$$ \vec{g} = -\vec{\nabla}\phi. $$
